Coach Sinclair has a vast experience at all levels of the game including High Performance Soccer, working in the NCAA, OUA, Oakville Soccer Club, Director of Coaching from u7-u8, in the OPDL with SC Toronto, as their OPDL High Performance Manager and currently with Whitby FC, as their OPDL BU17 Coach and League 1 Reserves U21.

Sinclair has extensive coaching background having served as assistant coach and recruiting coordinator with the University of Toronto women's soccer program. Ian previously served as an assistant coach with the McMaster Marauders men's soccer team from 2008-12, capturing OUA championships in 2009 and 2011. Sinclair was also an assistant with the Buffalo State College men's program NCAA, The State University of New York (Men) in 2005 and Brock University in 2004.

Coach Sinclair is a graduate of Niagara College's Recreation and Leisure Services program and he has also continued to expand his knowledge frequenting the National Soccer Coaches Association of America Convention, Ontario Soccer Association Coaching Conference and the National Soccer Coaching Conference.
To support you in your development to be better than your best on and off the field, Sinclair is excited to introduce his NEW private training programs for individuals and small groups.

Whether you are High performance player, competitive rep player, grassroots player (u8-u12), recreational player, University/College player or Pro player. This program is designed to challenge you to become a better player and help players acquire the necessary skills to take their game to the next level and everyone can get there.
